Knowledge Hub

Frequently Asked Questions

Understand how we structure your project and maintain security standards.

Why choose WordPress vs. Custom HTML?

Wordpress allows you to edit content yourself via a CMS, which is perfect for blogs or dynamic stores. Custom HTML is faster, more secure, and offers absolute design freedom.

What is the difference between SEO and SMO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) ranks your site naturally on Google search results. SMO (Social Media Optimization) configures profiles on Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram to funnel audience traffic to your site.

Why is an SSL Certificate critical?

An SSL certificate encrypts user interactions (like contact forms or credit card details) and displays the HTTPS secure lock. Google downranks websites that lack SSL protection.

How do automated backups protect my site?

Should a server outage or hardware failure occur, a clean restore database file from our daily automated backup ensures your site recovery is completed in minutes rather than days.
